— Service Four —

Naadi: Ancient Predictions

Ancient Indian sages, Rishis, wrote oracle messages for individuals along with remedies to help them lessen problems from Karma during this age of Kali Yuga. They also bring guidance and grace.

In south India, they are called 'Naadi' in Tamil, such as 'Agasthya Naadi'. In north India it is called 'Samhita', such as 'Brighu Samhita'. There are many sages who had these manuscripts written on various subjects in addition to personal predictions.

The reading of these old scripts on palm leaves is done by hundreds of readers all over India who have palm leaves bundles in their private libraries. They have been trained by their Guru or father as these leaves are passed down over generations. Many Naadi readers are not authentic, but I have managed to find genuine readers and helped many people find their leaf.

Not everyone finds their message on a leaf. But if you are destined to, you will find your leaf the very first time, and exactly when you are supposed to. A majority of the sages' scripts were destroyed by the Islamic invaders, or taken away by the British.

I had my Naadi reading in May, 2024, and it was life transforming to say the least. It inspired me to research and write a book on this topic. Most of this is still a mystery to me.

After accumulating knowledge and experience, I helped many friends and family through the Naadi process. Since my interview, hundreds of people contacted me to help them, this takes a lot of my time and energy, in exchange I do take a donation, and guide them in the process. It is my privilege to help you.

Note: For problems and obstacles the remedies are read. These remedies can include mantra chantings, sacred rituals (Pujas), and donations, to balance any Karma or negative influences with positive vibrations. If Pujas are prescribed, that can be done by the Naadi reader's Pandits at extra cost. It is up to you whether you want to do these remedies or not. I do NOT have any business or monetary relationship with any Naadi reader, they do not work for me, I only help you to get this done.
