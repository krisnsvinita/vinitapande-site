— About Vinita —

A bridge between ancient wisdom and modern life.

Vinita is a spiritual author. She published her third book Dance of Duality in 2024, based on a Kashmiri woman named Lalla and her mystical experiences.

In addition, Vinita offers spiritual guidance and is a certified Spiritual Life Coach, Past Life Regression and Future Progression therapist, and facilitator for the experience of Quantum Consciousness. She accepts a donation in exchange for her service.

Vinita is one of the world's leading experts in Naadi — ancient palm leaf manuscripts. These are prophecies written by the Maharishis of India thousands of years ago for specific individuals, about their past, present, and future, along with remedies to reduce difficulties.
